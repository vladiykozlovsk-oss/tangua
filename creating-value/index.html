Our approach

Three pillars that turn long-term supply into long-term value

Each partnership begins with a shared scorecard covering quality, emissions, availability, and cost. We revisit the scorecard quarterly to surface opportunities and mitigate risks together.

Strategic onboarding

Multi-disciplinary workshops map your regulatory obligations, plant configuration, and stakeholder priorities before the first shipment leaves our depot.

Data-driven improvement

Integrations with laboratory, SCADA, and logistics data create live dashboards. These insights drive corrective actions on blending, storage, and combustion curves.

Aligned governance

Joint steering groups review milestones, approve optimizations, and ensure sustainability claims withstand audits and investor scrutiny.

Value framework

A repeatable framework tailored to each client

  1. Phase 01

    Alignment & modelling

    We quantify business-as-usual costs, emission factors, and outage scenarios. The resulting model validates how biomass flows will support your net-zero pathway.

  2. Phase 02

    Execution sprint

    Dedicated teams oversee sourcing, transport, customs, and plant receipt. Weekly reviews capture performance data and document lessons learned.

  3. Phase 03

    Optimisation loop

    Predictive analytics recommend blends, back-up inventory, and hedging moves. We refresh the roadmap quarterly so decisions stay aligned with market change.
