About us

European biomass specialists building resilient supply chains

TANGUA A.S. unites forestry experts, energy analysts, and logistics coordinators under one mission: deliver low-carbon biomass reliably and responsibly for our partners.

Our story

From family-owned roots to a pan-European partner

We began as traders helping Central European sawmills monetise by-products. Today we manage integrated supply programmes across the Baltics, Nordics, and Balkans while keeping the same relationship-first mindset.

How we work

Local procurement teams stay embedded in their regions, building trust with forest owners and processors.

What drives us

We measure success in avoided emissions, dependable deliveries, and the growth of our partners.

Values

Principles that shape every partnership

Long-term commitment

We sign multi-year agreements that balance stability for suppliers with flexibility for customers.

Technical integrity

Our engineers validate every batch and continuously improve blends, storage, and combustion performance.

Shared accountability

Transparent dashboards and governance rituals keep stakeholders aligned on targets and progress.
