  • Origin
    Origin

    CM Biomass sources sunflower husk pellets from the primary producers in Balkan countries, such as Romania and Bulgaria, as well as Ukraine. These regions are known for their extensive sunflower oil and seed production, generating substantial quantities of husk as a byproduct.

  • Availability
    Availability

    Sunflower oil and seed producers have a long tradition of pelletizing the husk on-site for logistical and transportation purposes. This practice makes sunflower husk pellets widely available in the biomass market.

  • Sustainability
    Sustainability

    Over 95% of the biomass we supply originates from residue streams, including sawmills, vegetable oil production, and deshelling processes. By repurposing these materials, we support local waste management and provide an alternative
    energy source, reducing fossil fuel reliance.
